There are many genre of games. Let's take a look at a few of them!


Competitive Games

Competitive games are those in which players play against one another and where one player winning means another player loses. Two player games are often competitive, with a distinct win-or-lose outcome. Two-team games often have the same pattern, where one team winning means the other team loses. Example: Rocket League, Fornite, Call Of Duty and Apex Legends.



FPS games (first person shooter games)

First-person shooter is a sub-genre of shooter video games centered on gun and other weapon-based combat in a first-person perspective, with the player experiencing the action through the eyes of the protagonist and controlling the player character in a three-dimensional space. Example: Super Hot, Overwatch, Call of Duty and Halo.



Sandbox games

A sandbox game is a video game with a gameplay element that gives the player a great degree of creativity to complete tasks towards a goal within the game, if such a goal exists. Some games exist as pure sandbox games with no objectives. Exampls: Minecraft and roblox.

Indie / Horror Games

An independent video game or indie game is a video game typically created by individuals or smaller development teams without the financial and technical support of a large game publisher, in contrast to most "AAA" games. Example: Five Nights At Freddy's, Little Nightmares Series and undertale.
